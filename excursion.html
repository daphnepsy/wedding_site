Optional Excursions

Join us for curated day trips or explore on your own — ideas, logistics, and tips below.

Uxmal

Uxmal is a major Maya archaeological site — impressive pyramids and architecture. Typical visit: 2–4 hours.

Open in Google Maps

Tip: hire a licensed guide at the site or book a small-group tour from Mérida for transport + guide.

Cenote Dzombacal

Swim in a semi-open cenote with clear water. Bring towels, water shoes, sunscreen, and cash for entrance.
