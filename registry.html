Charitable Donations

In lieu of gifts, we'd love for you to consider donating to one of these organizations close to our hearts:

GiveWell

GiveWell is a nonprofit dedicated to finding the most effective giving opportunities in the world.

They conduct rigorous, independent research to identify charities where your donation can do the most good — helping save lives and improve wellbeing at remarkable scale.

Example high-impact charities they recommend include the Against Malaria Foundation, which provides insecticide-treated bed nets to prevent malaria in vulnerable communities, and the Schistosomiasis Control Initiative, which delivers treatment for parasitic infections that affect millions of people worldwide

Donations are tax-deductible in the US.

Feeding Hong Kong

A Hong Kong food bank established in 2011, Feeding Hong Kong fights food insecurity by rescuing surplus food and redistributing it to vulnerable communities across the city.

Donations are tax-deductible in the US.

Society for Community Organization (SoCO)

SoCO is a Hong Kong non-profit dedicated to social justice and advocacy for the disadvantaged. They provide housing support/legal assistance/community services to those facing homelessness and poverty.

One of Hong Kong's most devastating issues is its cage home and subdivided flat housing crisis, where low-income residents live in severely cramped, sweltering conditions — SoCO works in these communities to make residents' lives easier, and in parallel advocating for policy changes.
